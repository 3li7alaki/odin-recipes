Falafel Recipe

Description

Falafel is a popular vegetarian dish across the Middle East, made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, mixed with herbs and spices, and deep-fried until crispy. This recipe gives you delicious homemade falafel, perfect for serving in pita bread with vegetables and tahini sauce.

Falafel is commonly served with hummus, tabbouleh, and pickled vegetables. It's great as a main dish or a snack, packed with protein and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 400g dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Pita bread and tahini sauce for serving

Steps

  1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, cumin, and coriander. Pulse until you get a coarse mixture.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, and shape the mixture into small balls or patties.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan and fry the falafel until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Serve the falafel in warm pita bread, along with vegetables and tahini sauce.
  5. Enjoy your homemade falafel as a snack or main dish.
